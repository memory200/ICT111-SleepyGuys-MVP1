# Problem Notes: Campus Lost-and-Found System

## Target Respondent Breakdown
* **Group 1:** Campus Students (Primary users who lose or find items daily)
* **Group 2:** Security Staff & Receptionists (Primary operators who manage found items physically)

## Key Observed Pain Points
1. **Scattered Information Channels:** Information is fragmented across unofficial Telegram groups, Viber channels, and Facebook posts.
2. **Lack of Centralized Database:** Students have to physically walk around campus to multiple security desks to check for lost items.
3. **Unclear Item Verification:** Security staff struggle to verify the authentic owner of valuable lost items before returning them.

## Current Workarounds (Alternatives)
* Posting photos of found items on public class group chats.
* Walking directly to the main campus security desk or student affairs office.
* Leaving found items where they were spotted in hopes the owner returns.

## Consequences of Unsolved Problem
* **Financial Loss:** Students paying replacement fees for student IDs, access badges, and lost keys.
* **Wasted Time:** Hours spent asking around or walking to security hubs across campus.
* **Stress & Disruption:** Anxiety during exam weeks when critical study devices or materials are misplaced.
