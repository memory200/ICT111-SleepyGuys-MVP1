# Customer Discovery Summary

## Key Findings & Repeated Patterns
1. **Chat Group Overload:** 80% of interviewed students rely on Telegram/Viber groups where lost item reports are quickly buried and lost.
2. **Physical Search Friction:** Students waste substantial time walking between multiple security desks scattered across campus.
3. **Paper Log Limitations:** Security staff struggle to efficiently match lost reports with found items using manual paper books.

## Validated Problem Signals
* **Strong Need:** A central, searchable digital repository for lost items on campus is validated by both students and security personnel.
* **Core Feature Focus:** Search/filter by item category and photo upload capability are the highest requested capabilities.

## Next Steps for MVP Development
* Move forward with prototyping a **simple web-based dashboard** (HTML/CSS/JS or Figma) that allows searching and posting lost-and-found listings.
