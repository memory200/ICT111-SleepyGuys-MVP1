# Sales Scenario & User Context (Lab 13)

**Group Name:** SleepyGuys  
**Project:** Smart Campus Lost and Found System  

---

## 1. Target User Persona & Situation
* **User:** Aung Aung, a 2nd-year CS student at RSU.
* **Context:** Aung Aung lost his student ID card and laptop charger near the Central Library during mid-term exam week.
* **Pain Point:** He checked multiple Facebook groups, Telegram channels, and physical noticeboards, but found no centralized update. He is anxious about missing his exam without his ID.

---

## 2. Value Proposition & Pitch Moment
* **Sales Pitch:** "Instead of scrolling through hundreds of unorganized chat messages, use the SleepyGuys Smart Campus Lost & Found portal to instantly search by category, check verification status, or file a missing report in under 60 seconds."
* **Value Delivered:** Time saved, central accessibility, real-time status tracking, and identity-protected verification.
