# Customer Questions

## Project Title

Lost & Found System

## Target Respondent

* University Students
* University Staff

## Consent and Context

1. May we ask you a few questions for our ICT111 course project?
2. Your participation is voluntary. We will only use your responses for academic purposes. Is it okay if we take notes?

## Recent Experience

3. Have you ever lost a personal item on campus? If yes, what did you lose?
4. Can you describe what happened when you lost the item?
5. What steps did you take to try to find it?

## Current Workaround

6. How do you usually try to find a lost item on campus?
7. What methods do you use? (e.g., friends, social media, security office, campus staff)
8. What difficulties or frustrations did you face while trying to recover the item?

## Pain Severity and Consequence

9. How often do you lose or find items on campus?
10. How serious do you think this problem is on a scale of 1–5? Why?
11. What happens when a lost item cannot be recovered?

## Closing

12. Would you use a website or system that helps students report and find lost items?
13. Do you have any suggestions for improving the process of finding lost items on campus?
14. May we use your anonymous responses as evidence for our course project?
