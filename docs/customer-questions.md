# Customer Discovery Questions (Smart Campus Lost-and-Found System)

## Target Respondents
* University Students (Undergraduate / Postgraduate)
* Campus Security Personnel & Information Desk Staff
* Department Administrators

## Non-Leading Discovery Questions

### Section A: Understanding Past Experience & Frequency
1. Tell me about the last time you lost or mislaid a personal item on campus. What happened?
2. How often do you or your friends lose personal belongings on campus?
3. Have you ever found an item that belonged to someone else on campus? What did you do with it?

### Section B: Current Workarounds & Alternatives
4. When you lose an item now, what are the exact steps you take to search for it?
5. Which messaging groups, social media pages, or physical desks do you usually check first?
6. How effective have these channels been in helping you recover your items?

### Section C: Pain Points & Consequences
7. What is the most frustrating part of the process when trying to report or search for a lost item?
8. What consequence did you face when a lost item was not recovered (e.g., card re-issuance fee, missing classes)?
9. If you found an item today, what prevents you from turning it in to the security office right away?
