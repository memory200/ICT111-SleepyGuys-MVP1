# Critical Assumptions & Risk Matrix

| ID | Category | Assumption Statement | Risk Level | Evidence Level | Requirement ID |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **A-01** | User Problem | Campus users will prefer a web portal over informal chat groups to find lost items. | **High** | Low | FR-2.1 |
| **A-02** | Usability | Students can report a found item in under 2 minutes without staff help. | **High** | Medium | FR-1.1 |
| **A-03** | Value | Security desk collection details in the detail view will clear pickup confusion. | Medium | Low | FR-3.1 |
| **A-04** | Feasibility | Node.js/Express with local JSON/EJS storage can simulate real-time item status updates. | Low | High | NFR-1.1 |
| **A-05** | Business Logic | Security staff will actively mark items as `Claimed` in the management console. | Medium | Medium | FR-3.2 |
