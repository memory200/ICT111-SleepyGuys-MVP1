## Lab 02: IT Opportunity Scanning

### Activities Completed

During Lab 02, our team conducted IT opportunity scanning to identify potential semester project ideas. We reviewed ideas from Lab 01 and explored six real IT opportunities:

1. Lost & Found System
2. Online Food Ordering System
3. Student Attendance System
4. School Announcement System
5. Campus Navigation App
6. Library Book Search System

We analyzed each opportunity based on technology feasibility and identified the required tools, data, difficulty level, and whether the idea could be completed within the semester.

We then created a NUF (New, Useful, Feasible) Scoring Matrix to compare all opportunities systematically.

### Selected Opportunity

**Lost & Found System**

### Why It Was Selected

The Lost & Found System was selected because it received one of the highest NUF scores.

NUF Score:

- New = 3
- Useful = 5
- Feasible = 5
- Total = 13

Reasons for Selection:

- It solves a real problem commonly faced by students.
- Students frequently lose personal belongings on campus.
- There is currently no centralized platform for reporting and searching lost items.
- The project is highly useful for students and staff.
- The prototype can be developed using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript within the semester.
- The problem can easily be validated through interviews during Lab 03.

### Rejected Opportunity

**Campus Navigation App**

Reason for Rejection:

Although the idea is useful for new students, it requires map integration and additional technical complexity. The team determined that it would be more difficult to develop and test within the available semester timeline.
