# Initial IT Venture Idea Log

## Idea 1: Smart Campus Lost-and-Found System (Primary Focus)
### Problem Area
Students often lose personal belongings (IDs, laptops, keys, wallets) on campus and lack a centralized system to report or locate missing items.
### Target Users
University students, security staff, and administrative office.
### Current Alternative
Students post in informal social media chat groups or ask security guards manually.
### Proposed IT Solution
A web-based lost-and-found portal where users can report found items, search lost items, and contact administration.
### Possible Technology
HTML/CSS/JS prototype, Node.js server, image upload feature, and search/filter functionality.
### Why This is Suitable
It can be prototyped within a semester using core web development, database design, and user interface workflows.

## Idea 2: Campus Study Space & Facility Reservation
### Problem Area
Students waste time searching for empty study tables and discussion rooms during peak exam weeks.
### Target Users
University students, library staff, and study group leaders.
### Current Alternative
Students physically walk around campus to check for available seats.
### Proposed IT Solution
A real-time study space availability tracker and booking dashboard.
### Possible Technology
Interactive web dashboard, seat status database, and booking management system.
### Why This is Suitable
High practical demand on campus; easily testable with real students via UI/UX prototypes.

## Idea 3: Campus Food Court Pre-Ordering Platform
### Problem Area
Long queues at campus food stalls during lunch hours cause students to miss class or waste break times.
### Target Users
Campus students, university staff, and food court vendors.
### Current Alternative
Waiting in long physical queues or skipping meals.
### Proposed IT Solution
A simple web app for pre-ordering campus food and receiving pickup notifications.
### Possible Technology
Web-based ordering interface, vendor dashboard, and order status updates.
### Why This is Suitable
Clear problem-solution fit that demonstrates backend workflow and data management.
