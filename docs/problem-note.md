# Problem Notes

## Selected Case

Lost & Found System for university students and staff.

## Target Respondents

| Respondent Type     | Why This Respondent Matters                                     | Planned Number | Actual Number |
| ------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------: | ------------: |
| University Students | They are the main users who often lose or find items on campus. |             10 |            13 |
| University Staff    | They may help manage or return lost items.                      |              5 |             2 |

## Observed / Reported Problem

Many students lose personal belongings on campus and have difficulty finding them again. There is no central place to report or search for lost items.

## Current Alternatives or Workarounds

| Workaround                   | Who Uses It?       | Weakness / Pain                            | Evidence Source |
| ---------------------------- | ------------------ | ------------------------------------------ | --------------- |
| Social media posts           | Students           | Information is scattered and easy to miss. | Survey          |
| Asking friends               | Students           | Limited reach and often unsuccessful.      | Survey          |
| Checking security or offices | Students and Staff | Time-consuming and not always updated.     | Survey          |

## Repeated Pain Points

| Pain Point                       | Number of Mentions | Example Evidence                                              | Severity |
| -------------------------------- | -----------------: | ------------------------------------------------------------- | -------- |
| No central place for lost items  |                 10 | Respondents said they did not know where to report or search. | High     |
| Difficult to contact item owners |                  8 | Found items could not be returned easily.                     | Medium   |
| Searching takes too much time    |                  7 | Users had to check multiple places.                           | Medium   |

## Consequences

| Consequence                  | Example                                                   | Evidence Strength |
| ---------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------- |
| Lost items are not recovered | Students could not find lost wallets or ID cards.         | Strong            |
| Wasted time searching        | Users spent time checking different locations.            | Moderate          |
| User frustration             | Respondents felt stressed when important items were lost. | Moderate          |

## Notes for MVP Direction

The MVP should provide a simple way to:

* Report lost items
* Report found items
* Search for items quickly
* Contact the owner or finder
* Use a simple and mobile-friendly interface
