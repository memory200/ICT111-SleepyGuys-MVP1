# User Persona: Smart Campus Lost-and-Found System

## Primary Persona: Alex (The Busy Campus Student)

* **Age:** 20
* **Role:** Undergraduate Student (Faculty of IT)
* **Location:** Rangsit Campus
* **Tech Savviness:** High (Uses smartphone and web apps daily)

---

### Profile & Background
Alex is a full-time university student with a packed schedule of lectures, lab sessions, and group activities. He frequently moves between different campus buildings and cafeteria areas.

---

### Goals & Needs
* Needs a fast way to check if a missing item (e.g., Student ID, keys, airpods) has been found on campus.
* Wants a simple platform to post photos and details when he finds someone else's lost item.
* Wants clear status updates on whether an item has been returned or is stored at the security desk.

---

### Frustrations & Pain Points (Based on Lab 03 Evidence)
* **Chat Group Overload:** Messages in Telegram/Viber groups get buried in active chats within hours.
* **Wasted Time:** Walking across campus to check multiple physical security hubs manually.
* **Verification Anxiety:** Fear of losing critical items like Student IDs during exam weeks.

---

### Behavior & Preferences
* Prefers accessing services via a smartphone browser.
* Expects search/filter tools (by building, date, or category) rather than scrolling endlessly.
